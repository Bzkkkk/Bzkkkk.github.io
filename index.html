Publications

Balancing the Experts: Unlocking LoRA-MoE for GRPO via Mechanism-Aware Rewards
Changlian Ma, et al.
ICLR 2026

We propose RO-GRPO, a framework that addresses the router collapse problem in LoRA-MoE during reinforcement learning fine-tuning. By converting internal routing statistics into mechanism-aware rewards, RO-GRPO enables joint optimization without auxiliary losses, improving task accuracy, expert utilization, and inference efficiency across unimodal and multimodal math reasoning benchmarks.

InternVideo2.5: Empowering Video MLLMs with Long and Rich Context Modeling
Yi Wang, ..., Changlian Ma, et al.
Technical Report

InternVideo2.5 enhances multimodal LLMs for long video understanding and fine-grained visual perception through adaptive hierarchical token compression and visual task reward optimization.

Video-o3: Native Interleaved Clue Seeking for Long Video Multi-Hop Reasoning
Xiangyu Zeng, ..., Changlian Ma, et al.
Under Review at ICML 2026

Video-o3 introduces a native interleaved tool-calling mechanism with trajectory-guided reinforcement learning, enabling multimodal LLMs to autonomously seek clues and perform multi-hop reasoning in long videos, achieving SOTA on multiple complex video benchmarks.
